## **What is AWS?**

**AWS (Amazon Web Services)** is a **cloud computing platform** launched by **Amazon in 2006**.  
It provides **on-demand computing resources** — such as servers, storage, databases, networking, analytics, AI, and more — all **accessible via the internet**.
> AWS lets companies **rent IT infrastructure** instead of **buying and maintaining it.**

Main Idea : Rent IT services via Internet.
## **Why AWS?**
AWS became the **leader in cloud computing** because of its:
1. **Reliability** – used by Netflix, Airbnb, NASA, and government sectors.
2. **Scalability** – easily handle 10 users or 10 million users.
3. **Pay-as-you-go** – you pay only for what you use.
4. **Global presence** – data centers across 30+ regions and 100+ availability zones.
5. **Security** – top-level encryption, compliance, and certifications.
